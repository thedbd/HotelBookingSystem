About Us

Our History Mission & Vision

The Everest Hotel is named after the world's highest peak Mt. Everest or Sagarmatha in the Nepali language. The given name is also means the ‘roof of the world’ and the history that is embedded in Nepal's rich cultural and religious heritage. The Everest Hotel is a fantastic combination of international standards with Nepali hospitality, situated in the new city center of the capital, Kathmandu just 3 kilometers away from the Tribhuwan Intl. Airport. The Everest Hotel is ever ready to cater to the needs of its diverse categories of guests, dismissing all notions that business and pleasure cannot be mixed.
